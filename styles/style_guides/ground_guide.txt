GROUND TEXTURE RULES - SIMPLE VERSION:

This generates FLAT GROUND MATERIAL.

IMAGE = 100% GROUND SURFACE

"grass texture" = Imagine your camera is lying flat on the grass looking straight down. You see ONLY grass. Nothing else.

"dirt texture" = Camera flat on dirt. ONLY dirt. No stones, no roots, no objects.

"sand texture" = Camera flat on sand. ONLY sand. No shells, no shadows, no depth.

TILE = PATTERN = SURFACE = FLAT MATERIAL

NOTHING ELSE EXISTS IN THE IMAGE.

If you see sky, or trees, or horizon, or objects - THE GENERATION FAILED.
