HARD NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS - IMAGE REJECTION RULES:

REJECT GENERATION IF IMAGE CONTAINS:
- Sky, clouds, or any blue area
- Horizon line or "far away" view
- Multiple distinct objects
- Trees, bushes, buildings, or structures
- Any indication of depth or 3D perspective
- Any "interesting" or "focal" area
- Shadows that suggest sun/sky light source
- Any non-ground elements

IMAGEN COMPLETES SCENES BY DEFAULT.
YOUR JOB IS TO STOP THAT.

KEY PHRASES THAT TRIGGER BAD BEHAVIOR:
- "illustration" - DON'T USE
- "environment" - DON'T USE
- "scene" - DON'T USE
- "view" - DON'T USE
- "landscape" - DON'T USE

WHAT TO USE INSTEAD:
- "texture"
- "pattern"
- "surface"
- "material"
- "ground cover"

VISUAL REFERENCE:
Think of a seamless tileable PATTERN like a fabric swatch.
The image should look like CLOSE-UP GROUND MATERIAL.
Nothing more, nothing less.
