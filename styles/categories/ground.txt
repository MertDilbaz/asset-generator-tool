GROUND CATEGORY - STRICT RULES:

THIS CATEGORY PRODUCES TILES ONLY. TILES ARE NOT OBJECTS.

TILE RULES:
- The image is 100% ground material, 0% other
- Seamless tiling is MANDATORY
- Full-frame texture, NO focal point
- No objects, no decorations, no variations

ABSOLUTE RESTRICTIONS:
- NO STONES
- NO PEBBLES
- NO GRAVEL
- NO ROCKS
- NO SHELLS
- NO WEEDS
- NO FLOWERS
- NO TWIGS
- NO ANIMAL DROPPINGS
- NO MOSS
- NO VARIATION in color or texture unless explicitly in the asset name

TILE-ONLY COMMANDS:
"grass tile" = pure grass texture, uniform green
"dirt tile" = pure dirt texture, uniform brown
"sand tile" = pure sand texture, uniform tan
"mud tile" = pure mud texture, uniform dark brown

IF YOU WANT STONES/PEBBLES: The asset name MUST include "stones" or "pebbles"
IF YOU WANT GRASS BLADES: The asset name MUST include "grass blades" or "sparse grass"

OTHERWISE: Plain texture only, no variations.
